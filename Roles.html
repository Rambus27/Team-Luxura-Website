Every division, explained

Divisions

Our Structure

Team Luxura is made up of specialized divisions — each playing a critical role in building, competing, and representing our brand.

🏆

Competitive

The competitive division is the face of Team Luxura in organized play. These players represent the team in tournaments, leagues, and ranked ladders — demonstrating elite mechanics, game sense, and strategic coordination under pressure.

Tournaments Ranked Play Strategy
🏒

CCH

Custom Car Hockey (CCH) is a creative and competitive game mode where players use modified car physics to play hockey-style matches. CCH players combine mechanical skill with adaptability, mastering unconventional hitboxes and high-speed gameplay.

Custom Modes Mechanics Teamwork
🎮

Freestyler

Freestylers are the artists of the game. They dedicate themselves to mastering advanced mechanical tricks, aerial combos, and creative sequences in Rocket League's freeplay environment. Their clips define the team's highlight content and showcase the highest ceiling of individual skill.

Mechanics Creativity Freeplay
⚡

No Flipper

No Flipper is a specialized freestyle category where players are restricted from using the flip mechanic. This forces mastery of wave-dashing, half-flips, and alternative movement techniques — producing some of the most unique and technically impressive clips in the community.

No Flip Wave-Dash Technical
✨

VFX

The Visual Effects team creates dynamic motion graphics, particle animations, video transitions, and cinematic visual overlays for team content. VFX artists are responsible for the polished, energetic aesthetic that makes Team Luxura's videos stand out in a crowded space.

Motion Graphics Animation After Effects
🎨

GFX

Graphic Designers craft the static visual identity of Team Luxura. From social media banners and match-day graphics to thumbnails and team announcements, GFX artists ensure every piece of content is polished, on-brand, and visually striking.

Design Branding Photoshop
🎬

Content Creator

Content Creators produce, edit, and distribute video content across all of Team Luxura's social platforms. From gameplay montages and freestyle edits to highlight reels and promotional videos — they are the driving force behind the team's online presence and community growth.

Video Editing Social Media Storytelling
⚙️

Staff

Staff are the operational backbone of Team Luxura. Handling management, coordination, moderation, and day-to-day administration, they keep every division running smoothly. Without Staff, none of what Luxura accomplishes would be possible.

Management Coordination Operations
